Do i Qualify as an affiliate, Introducing Broker(IB),Broker or Tablet?

To ensure you register correctly, please note the following categories:

Affiliates:Promote Forex Brokers' products/services and receive commissions or CPA for each account opened

Introducing Brokers (IBs): Introduce new customers to Forex Brokers and receive a fee/commission

Brokers:Provide trading services to clients and promote these services on your website.

Traders:Individuals interested in or actively trading.
